Built By Boxers

Understanding first hand the highs and lows of the sport, our founders Bil Sha & Sedat Sag took it upon themselves to build the Revival Boxing empire and were later joined by Fayaz Masamyar as one of the promoters and match makers . Genuinely built from a passion for boxing, the trio decided to create a programme they could only dream about when they first took to the sport, encouraging a new generation of boxers to step into the ring in front of their friends and family. Whilst most importantly, a love for boxing.

Built By Boxers

There first show was hosted in London Irish Centre in October 2021 and was one that was talked about for months , helping boxers out of their own Gyms and developing them ready for fight night and there was nothing but success. Being in the sport for years they wanted to bring a difference how boxing is seen, making them one of the most unique white collar boxing promotions with careful matchmaking and unique training plans and best of promoting each fighter.
